# SkillSwap

> A structured peer-to-peer skill-sharing platform where everyone is both a learner and a mentor.

---

## What is SkillSwap?

SkillSwap is a dual-role skill exchange platform — not a course marketplace, not a tutoring listing. It's a structured infrastructure where users simultaneously learn one skill and teach another, earning through contribution and growing through structured progression.

---

## Core Philosophy

| Principle | Description |
|---|---|
| **Skill over status** | Anyone with competence can teach |
| **Contribution-driven growth** | Users are rewarded for sharing knowledge |
| **Structured learning** | Milestone-driven, not ad hoc sessions |
| **Dual-role identity** | Every user is both a learner and a mentor |

---

## What It Does

- 🔁 **Skill Exchange** — Match with peers to teach what you know, learn what you don't
- 🗺️ **Roadmap System** — Goal-based learning with milestone tracking and mentor suggestions
- 💳 **Credit Economy** — Earn credits by teaching; spend them to learn (no money required)
- 🤝 **Smart Matching** — Skill-aware matching based on level, availability, and roadmap stage
- 📊 **Growth Dashboard** — Track sessions, ratings, milestones, and learner requests in one place

---

## Skill Domains

**Academic** · **Technical** · **Extracurricular**

From Mathematics and Web Development to Guitar, Public Speaking, and Chess — SkillSwap is domain-agnostic but structured.

---

## How It Works

1. Register and set the skills you **offer** and the skills you want to **learn**
2. Get matched with peers aligned to your goals
3. Schedule a live session and pay via credits or directly
4. Complete the session, exchange ratings, and unlock credits
5. Track your progress through your active roadmap

---

## Vision

SkillSwap is built to evolve into a **campus-level skill exchange network** — a verified, contribution-based reputation system where collaborative growth replaces passive content consumption.
